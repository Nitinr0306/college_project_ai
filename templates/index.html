About This Project

Our Mission

We created this tool to help individuals understand their environmental impact and make more sustainable choices in their daily lives.

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time, and every action counts. By providing accessible tools and information, we hope to empower everyone to contribute to a more sustainable future.

Technologies Used

  • Python with Flask for the backend
  • HTML5 for structure
  • CSS with Tailwind for styling
  • JavaScript for interactivity
  • Ollama with Llama2 for AI assistance

Methodology

Our carbon footprint calculator provides an approximation based on widely accepted emission factors. While it doesn't capture every nuance of your environmental impact, it offers a good starting point for understanding and reducing your carbon footprint.

Emission Factors Used

  • Electricity: 0.5 kg CO2e per kWh (varies by region and energy source)
  • Transportation: Varies by vehicle type (0.192 kg CO2e/km for average gasoline car)
  • Diet: Ranges from 2.89 kg CO2e/day (vegan) to 7.19 kg CO2e/day (meat-heavy)

Disclaimer

This calculator provides estimates based on average values and simplified models. For a more precise measurement of your carbon footprint, consider professional carbon accounting services or more comprehensive tools.
